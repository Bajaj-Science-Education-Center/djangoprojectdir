Bajaj Science Education Centre was established in 2007, with Shri Rahul Bajaj as its president. Mr. C.K Desai served as its founder Director till 2015 and laid the foundations of its ‘learning-by-doing’ philosophy.
The Centre is spread over an area of 3000 m2. It has six laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, Electronics and Computers, Astronomy). Each laboratory is equipped for carrying out the prescribed experiments and also has the pedagogic tools and teaching aids required for imparting learning. The Centre also houses a library, an auditorium, an exhibition hall and an open-air amphitheatre.
